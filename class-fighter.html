Fighter

The martial professional whose skill sustains every divine faction's ground operations — serving Tempus's Order of the Iron Gauntlet, Bane's Black Fist military branches, or operating as an independent contractor whose capabilities are courted by every power that needs a capable sword and cannot afford to ask where its loyalties currently lie.

Setting Role

Action Surge — an additional full action once per short rest — makes the Fighter Aethrya's highest single-round damage output option. Extra Attack scales from two attacks at Adept to three at Expert, giving each mastery tier a meaningful combat capability increase. Indomitable at Expert allows rerolling failed saves, addressing the Fighter's primary vulnerability to spell effects.

The Battle Master's Superiority Dice system is the most tactically flexible Fighter option — Maneuvers like Riposte, Menacing Attack, and Commander's Strike translate into social and narrative capability as well as combat advantage, making the Battle Master the most narratively versatile Fighter build. The Order of the Iron Gauntlet trains all commissioned officers in Battle Master techniques, and Aldric Brand's personal assessment of promising officers is known to reflect Battle Master capability more than raw combat power.

Fighters serving the Order of the Iron Gauntlet access Tempus's professional military network — intelligence on current conflicts, access to military-grade equipment through official channels, and Aldric Brand's direct attention at Expert mastery. Fighters serving the Black Fist access Bane's institutional efficiency and the Interior Ministry's administrative reach. Fighters serving neither discover that truly independent skilled professionals are the rarest and most coveted commodity in Aethrya's current political climate.
