Profession: Cook

The profession that sustains the bodies that sustain the world — whose outputs benefit every other character on the server, whose social role makes them one of the most naturally connected characters in any community, and whose relationship to Chauntea's domain is the most intimate of any non-divine profession.

Cook
Sustaining ProfessionCommunity RoleHarvest Fellowship Adjacent

Primary Skills: Cook's Utensils · Herbalism Kit · Persuasion
Key Output: Meals · Long Rest benefits · Morale bonuses · Community gathering support
Divine Connection: Chauntea's Harvest Fellowship · Yondalla's community tradition

Cooks provide mechanical benefits through prepared meals that extend their effects into the subsequent Long Rest — temporary HP, stabilised wound recovery, enhanced morale. At Master tier, a Cook's feast produces effects that no other profession can replicate through their output alone. The social dimension of cooking — the gathering of people around a shared table — makes Cooks among the most naturally relationship-building characters in any settlement, and Chauntea's connection to the sustaining traditions of mortal life means that Maren Seedhallow has personal interest in the professional development of Master-tier Cooks. It is not unheard of for Maren to appear at the right moment with cultivation advice that improves an ingredient's quality in inexplicable ways.
