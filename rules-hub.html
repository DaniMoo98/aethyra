Restricted Activities in Hub Zones

Within declared hub city boundaries, the following are restricted: combat initiated without provocation, mind-affecting magic cast on non-consenting targets in public spaces, visible display of insignia from factions in active conflict with the hub compact, and construction or structural modification without plot registration approval. What is expressly permitted: passive factional affiliation, private meetings in registered premises, trade of restricted goods through licensed merchants, and personal utility magic.
