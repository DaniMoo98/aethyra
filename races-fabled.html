Fabled Races

The most extraordinary character options in Aethrya — including the Pantheon Race, which allows a player to embody a hidden god themselves. Each Fabled race represents a categorical shift in the kind of story a character tells and the weight of responsibility that character carries within the world.

Pantheon Race ✦
Become one of Aethrya's hidden gods — walk in mortal form under the Veil Accord, maintain your disguise through any challenge, accumulate divine power through Mastery tiers, and shape the world's story from the inside of the most carefully constructed narrative position on the server. The highest-demand Fabled option and the most demanding to play well.
Cambion
The mortal children of fiend and humanoid unions — bearing multiple fiendish features, carrying the mark of infernal heritage in their appearance and capabilities, and navigating a world that has very strong reactions to both. Their arc is defined by the question of what heritage means when the bearer had no voice in acquiring it.
Mind Flayer
Illithid — psionic aberrations of the deep places, ancient enemies of the Gith races, and the most cognitively alien playable character in Aethrya. A Mind Flayer character among mortals is a continuous exercise in inhabiting a consciousness that experiences the world through fundamentally different sensory and relational frameworks. The most demanding Fabled role for sustained, consistent characterisation.
True Vampire
Full vampiric transformation — ageless, capable of creating Fledgeling vampires, associated with Vorthax's undead intelligence network, and under continuous Judicator monitoring. The only Fabled race that begins its arc already known to a specific divine agent, creating an immediate and inescapable divine entanglement from the first scene.

All Fabled races require the full application process described in the Fabled Race Expectations page — written backstory, Lore Team interview, and onboarding documentation. Applications are reviewed weekly. See #fabled-applications in Discord for current wait times and submission requirements.
