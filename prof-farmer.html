Professions Index

The full roster of Aethrya's playable professions — each with their mastery track, key outputs, and divine connections. Select a profession below to view its full page.

Select your profession at character creation. Profession mastery advances independently of class mastery. A character may hold only one active profession; profession change follows the Class & Profession Change Rules and resets mastery to Novice. Choose something that makes sense for your character's life before they became an adventurer — the backstory you build around your profession is as important as the mechanical output it provides.
