A Zealot Barbarian who investigates the true source of their divine Rage will find that the answer traces to something older and stranger than any recognised pantheon — and that the Primordial Titan awakening arc runs directly through them.

Setting Role & The Primordial Thread

Rage is Aethrya's most powerful sustained combat state — providing a damage bonus, resistance to physical damage, advantage on Strength checks and saves, and immunity to the charmed and frightened conditions. Reckless Attack offers the server's highest-risk, highest-reward baseline attack option: advantage on all attack rolls for a round at the cost of giving attackers advantage against you. The combination of these features makes Barbarians capable of combat output that no other class matches at consistent deployment.

The Zealot Barbarian's divine Rage damage, the Ancestral Guardian's protective ancestral spirits, the Totem Warrior's spirit guides — these mechanics invoke divine-adjacent forces without belonging to any recognised pantheon's institutional framework. The Veil Accord was constructed to govern gods operating in mortal form; it was not constructed to address mortals who access primordial power through non-institutional channels. This legal gap has never been formally adjudicated by the Judicators. A Zealot Barbarian who investigates the divine source of their Rage will find that it traces to the primordial war-force that powered the Primordial Titans' direct engagement with mortal civilisation during the Age of Revelation — and that the Titan awakening unfolding in the current season flows through the Zealot's class mechanics in ways that no other class can access through the same origin. This investigation thread is the most unique and most narratively consequential character arc the Barbarian class offers.
