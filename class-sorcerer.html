Sorcerer

Your magic flows from within — from bloodline, from cosmic circumstance, from proximity to forces whose scale you may never fully comprehend. Mystra and Shar have both written research papers on what it means that divine power can bleed into mortal bloodlines through centuries of proximity to hidden gods. Neither has published their findings. Both have noted your name.

Metamagic & Subtle Power

The Metamagic system — Twin, Empower, Quicken, Extend, Heighten, or Subtle — creates Aethrya's most creatively flexible magical toolkit. No other class can modify spells in these ways, and this capacity for adaptation makes Sorcerers the most inventive casters available in scenarios that reward improvisation over preparation.

Subtle Spell, which removes somatic and verbal components from a spell's execution, makes a cast completely invisible as spellcasting to any observer. This carries profound implications for the Charm and Dominate Rules consent framework, for investigation mechanics, and for the particular operating style of Velara Dusk's counselling practice — which the DM team does not draw attention to at character creation, but which Sorcerer characters discover at Adept mastery with a specificity that is not coincidental.

The Divine Soul subclass is theologically anomalous in Aethrya's framework — a character channelling divine power without belonging to any deity's institutional structure. Whether this origin represents bloodline accident, deliberate divine operation outside traceable religious networks, or evidence of divine power leaking into mortal heritage through centuries of proximity to hidden gods is the central question of this subclass's Expert-tier story arc. Both Mystra and Shar have theories. The character's arc is the evidence that determines which theory is correct.
