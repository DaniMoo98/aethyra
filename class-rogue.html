Rogue

In a world built on hidden agendas and carefully constructed ignorance, your skill set is not merely useful — it is the primary challenge every divine network must solve before it can operate safely. The Veilbreakers recruit heavily from your tradition. So does the Children of the Spider Queen. Both know why.

Setting Role & The Impostor Problem

Rogues possess a natural relationship to every faction operating in the shadows — which, in Aethrya, is every faction. Expertise doubles proficiency bonus in chosen skills, creating the server's highest ceiling for Stealth, Deception, Perception, and Investigation checks. Reliable Talent at Master mastery treats any roll below 10 as a 10 — making a Master Rogue's expertise skills essentially incapable of catastrophic failure.

The Assassin's Impostor feature — studying a creature and replicating their appearance, speech, and mannerisms well enough to pass mundane inspection — is one of the few mortal capabilities that approaches divine disguise quality. The Judicators reviewed two incidents in the last century where an Assassin Impostor was mistaken for a divine mortal-form disguise by investigators specifically looking for one. The Judicators declined to intervene, for reasons that remain on record as "insufficient cause established." The Veilbreakers have noted this in their file on the Judicators.
