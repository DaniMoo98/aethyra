Fabled race applications require an established record on the server first. How does the mastery system work in practice?â–¶

You begin at Novice. Advancing to Adept requires attending three DM sessions, demonstrating your character's personality and factional role consistently, and receiving DM team confirmation. Post the advancement request in #mastery-advancement with links to relevant scene logs. The DM team typically responds within one week. Expert and Master follow the same process with elevated requirements â€” see the Mastery Progression page for full details.

What if I disagree with a DM ruling?â–¶

DM rulings during active scenes are final in the moment â€” do not contest them in scene. After the scene concludes, submit a question or concern through #mod-mail or a private staff ticket. Senior staff review disputed rulings within 72 hours. Rulings confirmed as errors are corrected retroactively where possible. The appeal process exists for genuine errors, not for outcomes you disliked.

How does PvP work?â–¶

Player-versus-player combat requires: established IC motivation, notification to the DM team before the conflict begins, OOC acknowledgment from both parties that PvP is occurring, and compliance with the consent framework. Unprovoked ambush-style PvP requires DM pre-approval. PvP death is permanent only with DM approval. The Combat Rules page covers the full PvP protocol.

Arcane magic flows through Mystra's Weave â€” the living divine framework she maintains. It is subject to Weave conditions, requires Church of the Seven Stars registration, and cannot create divine disguises because it is Weave-dependent. Divine magic flows through a direct patron connection â€” it is not Weave-dependent, but the deity on the other end is always aware of its use. Cantrips, Novice, Adept, Expert, and Master tiers apply to both systems. The Magic & The Weave page covers the full system.
