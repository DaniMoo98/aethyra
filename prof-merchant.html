Profession: Merchant

From stall to empire — the Merchant's mastery path is Aethrya's most direct route from individual enterprise to cross-faction economic diplomacy, and the profession most closely watched by Vergadain's trade network.

Merchant
TradeInformation EconomyCross-Faction

Primary Skills: Persuasion · Insight · Economics · Appraisal
Key Output: Trade income · Faction trade contracts · Market intelligence
Divine Connection: Vergadain's Bright Bargain network · Tymora's House of Many Chances

At Novice, a Merchant operates a stall. At Adept, a permanent shop. At Expert, multi-location commercial operations and faction trade contracts. At Master, their accumulated relationship networks and commercial standing allow direct cross-faction economic negotiation — a capability that, in Aethrya's divine political landscape, places them at the intersection of every major power's economic interests simultaneously. Kessa Brightfall is known to favour merchants who take bold positions. The House of Many Chances occasionally finances exceptional commercial ventures. Both connections are noted in the Merchant's master-tier advancement documentation.
