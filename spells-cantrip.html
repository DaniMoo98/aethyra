Cantrips & Fluff Magic

Zero-cost magical expressions available to all spellcasters — the ambient magic of daily life in Aethrya. In a world where every arcane cast is drawn through Mystra's living Weave, even the smallest thread is observed. Nothing is truly incidental.

Cantrips cost 0 SP and are available to all spellcasters at any mastery tier. They represent the most practised, most automatic expressions of a spellcaster's connection to their magical source — muscle memory translated into magic. In Aethrya, cantrips have a particular cultural role: they are the visible layer of magical ability that non-magical characters observe, and their presence or absence in a character's daily behaviour is often the first indicator to observant NPCs that magical capability is present at all.

Fluff Magic encompasses non-combat utility cantrips — small magical expressions that serve narrative and atmospheric rather than mechanical purposes. Characters may use fluff magic freely without tracking costs. It enriches scenes, establishes magical presence, and creates the texture of a world where magic is woven into ordinary life.

GuidanceDivinationCleric, Druid+1d4 to one ability check. The most used support cantrip on the server, cast on virtually every significant skill check. Chauntea's Harvest Fellowship Clerics cast it reflexively on almost every ally action.
Toll the DeadNecromancyCleric, Warlock, WizardNecrotic damage — heightened against injured targets. The Doomguide Order's combat cantrip of choice. In the hands of Casker Morvaine's staff, its use in a hub city constitutes an extremely pointed statement.

Every arcane cantrip cast in the Throne of Ashes is, in a technical sense, observed by Mystra's ongoing Weave monitoring. She does not actively attend every Prestidigitation cast in every tavern. But the Church of the Seven Stars' arcane census captures patterns across all registered casters — frequency of use, spell school preference, casting hour — and these patterns are reviewed by Tessara Wand's network analysts weekly. The doctoral student's cantrip casting pattern was flagged fourteen months ago. It took this long for the monitoring recommendation to reach the relevant divine agents.
