Professions Index

Aethrya profession play is structured as a full economy loop: gatherers secure supply, crafters convert value, and logistics professions move goods through faction channels. The active profession roster below matches the current live wiki pages and mod support set.

Each character selects one profession identity. Profession mastery is independent from class mastery and is handled through RPR finalize flow and profession perk assignment. Profession changes should follow your reset rules and remove prior recipe bundles before applying new ones.

Market governance: keep raw-material pricing controlled (recommended cap: 50% of NPC buy value unless contract terms justify exceptions) to prevent runaway inflation and preserve faction logistics gameplay.
